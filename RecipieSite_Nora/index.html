Welcome to Nora's Pasta Salad recipe!

This is a recipe my mother has made us ever since I was younger - I've decided to share it with the world.
This feeds about 4 people.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Farfelle pasta: 1 box
  • Peeled, Deveined Shrimp: 1 pound
  • Minced Garlic: 3-4 cloves
  • Olive Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt & Pepper: to taste
  • Diced Tomatos: 1 tomato
  • Minced Parsley: 1/4 of a bundle
  • Lemon Juice: 3 tablespoons (or to taste)
  • Feta Cheese Crumbles: 3/4 of a cup (or to taste)

Directions

  1. First, Put a pan on the stove and heat up the olive oil.
  2. Once the olive oil is hot, place all the shrimp in the pan, with the salt and pepper.
  3. As the shrimp begins to cook, add the garlic in.
  4. Move the shrimp around every so often so that its all evenly sauteed.
  5. Once the shrimp is evenly browned, place it to the side.
  6. Place the following ingredients in a large bowl:
    • the pasta
    • diced tomatos
    • minced parsley
  7. Now, mix in the shrimp.
  8. Pour the lemon juice in the bowl and mix.
  9. Finally, throw in the Feta Cheese (don't be shy with it).
  10. Mix it all up and enjoy!
